House of Orphans and Asnaf Qaseh Ibu

Rumah Qaseh Ibu houses a total of 60 children from various backgrounds such as orphans, asnaf children, converts, illegitimate children and even abandoned children.The home is located at No. 482, Taman Lembah Jaya, Ampang, with a total floor space of approximately 14,000 sq ft.

"Your Concern Creates Smiles on the Faces of Children."

The children are introduced to a new family under the supervision of house parents, where they form bonds with other 'brothers' and sisters in a caring atmosphere. A balanced regimen of education, chores, play, and relaxation brings them delight and stability. Orphaned, mistreated, and abandoned children are given the chance to grow up and fulfil their full potential thanks to community well-wishers ranging from people to companies and non-profit organisations that join with Qaseh Ibu House in our objective. Many of these children grow up in the house, finish their school, and are empowered to lead productive lives in the community, protected from the violence, cruelty, and neglect they formerly knew.
