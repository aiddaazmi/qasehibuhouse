Organization of Orphans and Asnaf Qaseh Ibu officially registered with the Department Registration of Malaysian Organizations (JPPM) on 02 October 2018 and this organization known as Rumah Qaseh Ibu. The registration number is PPM-008-10-02102018. This organization is also registered under the Department of Social Welfare (JKM) and deals either with the protector or JKM officer of the Hulu Langat branch. Mother's House houses 60 children consisting of orphans, orphans, converted children, illegitimate children and even abandoned children. Rumah Qaseh Ibu is located at lot 482, Jalan Sri Kemuning, Lembah Jaya Selatan, 68000 Ampang, Selangor.

Vission

Educate and shape personality children in the aspects of religious, manners and good morals and pure values ​​in life so that they become individuals who will be successful in the future.

Mission

Give protection, pour out unconditional love and create a family atmosphere to these children even though they have different family background, race, ethnicity and nationalities.

Objectives

Provide a comfortable living environment with facilities such as men's bedrooms and girls, baby and children's room, study room, dining room and recreation room.

Providing academic and religious education level in primary, secondary, college and university so they able to be independent and get a job in the future later.

Take children to nearby clinics and hospitals for get health care so that they are protected from various diseases.

Giving awareness to the community tthrough annual programmes organized by Rumah Qaseh Ibu management.
